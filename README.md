#  01 Mutual-Fund-Risk-Return-Analysis

Overview:
---
Nippon India Growth Mid Cap Fund is a long-standing mid-cap scheme that has delivered consistent long-term compounding over the years. While it may not always dominate category discussions, its track record reflects steady wealth creation. This project analyzes the fund across risk-adjusted returns, sector allocation, portfolio positioning, and benchmark performance to understand the drivers behind its outcomes. The objective is to evaluate the fund beyond headline returns by examining risk, valuation profile, and structural exposure.

Evaluation & Analytical Framework:
This project follows a structured approach to assess the fund across performance, risk, and portfolio dimensions. The aim is to move beyond basic return figures and evaluate how efficiently returns are generated relative to benchmark and category peers. The analysis integrates quantitative performance metrics with portfolio-level insights to assess consistency and risk-adjusted efficiency.

The evaluation includes:

* Return analysis through CAGR and SIP performance across multiple tenures
* Risk metrics including Sharpe ratio, Beta, Alpha, and volatility
* Performance comparison with Nifty Midcap 150 TRI and BSE Sensex
* Sector allocation breakdown to assess exposure and concentration
* Portfolio valuation and growth characteristics such as P/E, P/B, earnings and sales growth
* Review of portfolio changes over recent quarters to understand active management decisions


Key Insights:
---
The fund has delivered a 5-year CAGR of 30.40%, outperforming its benchmark across longer holding periods. Risk-adjusted performance remains strong, with a Sharpe ratio of 1.65 and positive alpha of 2.76, indicating efficient return generation relative to volatility. Sector allocation shows higher exposure to Financials at 25.19%, reflecting a cyclical tilt while maintaining diversification across other sectors. Overall, the portfolio structure supports long-term growth orientation.
